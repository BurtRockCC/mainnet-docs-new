# Bridging sUSD

### Bridging from Ethereum Mainnet to Optimism

Bridging from Ethereum Mainnet to Optimism with zero slippage is possible through the Synthetix Native Bridge, which takes \~5 minutes to process.

1. Navigate to [https://legacy-staking.synthetix.io/bridge](https://legacy-staking.synthetix.io/bridge).

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-07 at 2.43.37 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Approve and initiate the withdrawal.
3. After a few minutes, sUSD will arrive in your wallet on Optimism.

### Bridging from Optimism to Ethereum Mainnet

Bridging from Optimism to Ethereum Mainnet with zero slippage is possible through the Synthetix Native Bridge, which takes 7 days to prove and process.

1. Navigate to [https://legacy-staking.synthetix.io/bridge](https://legacy-staking.synthetix.io/bridge).

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-07 at 2.24.39 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Approve and initiate the withdrawal to Mainnet.&#x20;
3. After \~1 hour, navigate to [https://superchainrelayer.xyz/](https://superchainrelayer.xyz/) or [https://www.brid.gg](https://www.brid.gg/)[/](https://www.brid.gg/) and input your transaction hash from the withdrawal transaction.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-02-07 at 2.33.31 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. After 7 days, finalize the withdrawal using the same site [https://superchainrelayer.xyz/](https://superchainrelayer.xyz/) or [ https://www.brid.gg](https://www.brid.gg).
